## COLOR PALETTE

### Dark Theme (Matches Your App)
- **Background**: #1a1a1a (Dark gray) or #000000 (Black)
- **Primary Text**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Secondary Text**: #CCCCCC (Light gray)
- **Accent**: #3b82f6 (Blue) or #f97316 (Orange)
- **Highlights**: #10b981 (Green for checkmarks)

### Light Theme (Professional)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Primary Text**: #1f2937 (Dark gray)
- **Secondary Text**: #6b7280 (Medium gray)
- **Accent**: #2563eb (Blue)
- **Highlights**: #059669 (Green)

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Font Recommendations
1. **Sans-serif**: Arial, Helvetica, Roboto, Open Sans
2. **Modern**: Inter, Poppins, Montserrat

### Font Sizes (Guideline)
- **Title**: 48-60pt (Bold)
- **Subtitle**: 24-32pt (Regular/Semi-bold)
- **Section Headers**: 20-24pt (Bold)
- **Body Text**: 16-18pt (Regular)
- **Tech Stack**: 14-16pt (Regular, Gray)

## POWERPOINT TEMPLATE STEPS

1. **Create New Slide**
   - Layout: Blank or Title Only
   - Size: 16:9 (Standard)

2. **Add Title**
   - Font: Arial Bold, 54pt
   - Color: White (dark bg) or Dark gray (light bg)
   - Position: Top center

3. **Add Subtitle**
   - Font: Arial, 28pt
   - Color: Light gray
   - Position: Below title

4. **Add Content Boxes**
   - Use rectangles with rounded corners
   - Fill: Slight transparency (10-20%)
   - Border: Accent color, 2-3pt
   - Padding: 15-20pt

5. **Add Features List**
   - Use checkmark icons (âœ…)
   - Font: 18pt, Regular
   - Spacing: 1.2 line height
   - Alignment: Left

6. **Add Tech Stack**
   - Font: 14pt, Regular
   - Color: Medium gray
   - Position: Bottom
   - Separator: â€¢ (bullet) or | (pipe)

7. **Final Touches**
   - Check alignment (use guides)
   - Test readability from 3m away
   - Ensure contrast is good
   - Remove clutter

## PRESENTATION TIPS

1. **Practice**: Time yourself - exactly 1 minute
2. **Energy**: Speak with confidence and enthusiasm
3. **Eye Contact**: Look at audience, not just slide
4. **Pointing**: Use gestures to highlight key points
5. **Pace**: Don't rush - clear and deliberate
6. **Closing**: End with "Thank you" or "Questions?"

---

## BACKUP PLAN

- If technology fails: Have slide exported as PDF/image
- If time runs short: Skip tech stack, focus on features
- If asked to elaborate: Be ready with 30-second answers
- If nervous: Breathe, smile, remember you know your project
